1. Cancellation Window

Cancellation requests must be submitted within 24 hours of making a contribution. After this window, contributions are considered final and non-cancellable as funds are immediately allocated to film production activities.

2. How to Request Cancellation

To request a cancellation within the 24-hour window, please contact us immediately at:
Email: projectmaya2026@gmail.com
Please include your name, email address, payment ID, and amount in your cancellation request.

3. Cancellation Processing Time

Once a valid cancellation request is approved, the refund will be processed within 7–10 working days to your original payment method.

4. Non-Cancellable Contributions

Contributions made more than 24 hours ago cannot be cancelled. This policy is necessary to ensure the continuity of film production activities funded by your contribution.

5. Failed Transactions

If your payment failed but your account was debited, the amount will be automatically refunded within 5–7 working days by the payment gateway. Please contact us if the refund does not reflect within this period.
